Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a circle?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen, enter a name for your circle, and tap "Create Circle". You'll automatically be added as the owner.
How do I invite someone to my circle?
Open your circle, tap the "ðŸ‘¥" button, then tap "Create Invite Link". Share the link or QR code with the person you want to invite.
How do I post an update?
Open your circle, tap the "+" button at the top, write your update, optionally add a photo, and tap "Post Update".
Can I leave a circle?
Yes, members can leave a circle at any time. Open the circle, go to Members, and tap "Leave Circle". Note: Owners must transfer ownership before leaving.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All updates are encrypted, and only members of your circle can see your updates. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data.
How do I change my password?
Go to Settings (tap your profile icon), then tap "Change Password" and follow the instructions.
