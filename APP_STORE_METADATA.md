Perfect for:

• Friends staying updated on health journeys
• Anyone needing a private, secure way to share updates

Privacy & Security:
• All updates are encrypted
• Only circle members can see updates
• Secure invite system
• Your data stays private

Download Care Circle today and keep your care network connected.

## Content Guidelines Compliance

### Health & Medical Apps
- Must comply with HIPAA if handling PHI
- Must have clear privacy policy
- Cannot make medical claims
- Must be clear about data usage

### Privacy Requirements
- Privacy policy must be accessible
- Must explain data collection
- Must explain data usage
- Must comply with GDPR (if serving EU users)

## Submission Process

### iOS
1. Create app in App Store Connect
2. Fill out all metadata
3. Upload screenshots and assets
4. Submit for review
5. Wait for approval (typically 24-48 hours)

### Android
1. Create app in Google Play Console
2. Fill out store listing
3. Upload screenshots and assets
4. Complete content rating
5. Submit for review
6. Wait for approval (typically 1-3 days)
